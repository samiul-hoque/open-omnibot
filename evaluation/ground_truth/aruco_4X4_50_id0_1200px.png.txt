aruco marker print spec
-----------------------
dict:     4X4_50 (cv2.aruco.DICT_4X4_50)
id:       0
side_px:  1200
border:   1 module (OpenCV default; detector relies on this)
polarity: black modules on white background (do NOT invert)
commit:   a7f1aa7

Print at 10 cm physical side on matte paper. Keep >= 5 mm white
quiet zone around the marker. Do not laminate (specular glare
kills detection).

Detector must match: aruco_detector.py DEFAULTS['marker_id'] = 0
and build_detector() DICT_4X4_50.
